## About
Creative Robotics Hub led by the University of the Arts London through the Creative Computing Institute, dedicated to exploring the intersection of robotics and creativity. The hub brings together artists, designers, technologists, and researchers to develop innovative projects that merge engineering with artistic expression. By combining creative practice with advanced robotics, Creative Robotics Hub aims to push the boundaries of how machines can collaborate with humans in areas such as performance, design, and interactive experiences.
